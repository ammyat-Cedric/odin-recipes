Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken Katsu is a Japanese-style fried chicken cutlet that is breaded with panko breadcrumbs and deep-fried until crispy. It is often served with tonkatsu sauce, shredded cabbage, and steamed rice. This dish is easy to make and offers a delicious combination of textures and flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable or peanut oil for frying
  • Tonkatsu sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Shredded green cabbage and sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken
  2. Prepare the breading
  3. Bread the chicken
  4. Fry the chicken
  5. Drain and slice the chicken
  6. Serve with tonkatsu sauce, shredded cabbage, and steam rice, and enjoy!
